**Badge** — compact pill label for status, category, or count.

```jsx
<Badge tone="success">Aktiv</Badge>
<Badge tone="accent" variant="solid">Neu</Badge>
<Badge tone="sky" variant="outline" size="sm">HAK</Badge>
```

Tones: `neutral` (default), `primary`, `accent`, `highlight`, `sky`, `success`, `warning`, `error`, `info`. Variants: `soft` (default), `solid`, `outline`. Sizes: `sm`/`md`/`lg`.
